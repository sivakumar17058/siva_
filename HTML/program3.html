Tomato Chutney

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. In a mixer jar add the onion, tomato and blend it to a fine paste.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a kadai, pour oil and add mustard seeds, urad dal and let it crackle.

    Now add the onion tomato paste into the kadai.

    Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, salt and cook it for 10 minutes until the raw smell goes out.

Coriander Chutney

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Saute the dals in oil until golden. Add the remaining ingredients except the coriander and saute until soft.

  2. Step 2

    3. Finally add the coriander leaves and turn off the flame. Cool down slightly and blend into a smooth paste with some water.

  3. Step 3

    4. For the tempering: splutter mustard seeds in the oil and add the remaining ingredients. Cook further for 20 seconds. Add this tempering into the chutney paste and mix well.

IdliPodi

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Dry roast the chana dal and urad dal on a low flame for 4 minutes until browned and set aside.

  2. Step 2

    3. In the same pan, dry roast the chillies until crisp for about a minute in a medium flame and keep with the roasted dal.

  3. Step 3

    4. Roast the cumin and sesame seeds until aromatic, 4 minutes on a low flame. And add salt and asafoetida to the bowl and mix well.

  4. Step 4

    5. Transfer all the roasted ingredients to a mixer grinder, add the salt and blend to a fine powder. Store the IdliPodi in an air-tight container.
